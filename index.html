My Process

When taking a project from beginning to end, I follow a process with 4 basic steps: research, prototype, testing/iterating, and finally, visual design.

Research

  • Brainstorming
  • Team meetings
  • Moodboards
  • Sketches
  • Blueprints

Testing/Iterating

  • Usability tests
  • User experience
  • Editing/updating
  • Experimenting

Prototype

  • User flow
  • Basic visuals

Visual Design

  • High Fidelity
  • Finalize assets
  • Accessiblity
